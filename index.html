Running Tips

Strength

Strength training is an important part of injury prevention. Focus on your core, abs, and glutes.

Cadence

Check your stride turnover. The most efficent runners take about 180 steps per minute.

Change it up

Don't run the same every time you hit the road. Vary your pace, and vary the distanc of your runs.

Focus on Form

Run tall but relaxed. Your feet should hit the ground beneath your hips, not out in front of you.
